Airport Express Policies

Please review the following service policies before booking. By scheduling transportation with Airport Express, you acknowledge and agree to these terms.

1. Cancellation Policy

24-Hour Risk-Free Cancellation
Customers may cancel their booking within 24 hours of the original reservation time and receive a full refund, provided the scheduled trip is more than 24 hours away at the time of booking. If the trip is scheduled to occur within 24 hours of booking, the reservation is non-refundable.

Cancellations More Than 24 Hours Before Scheduled Service
Cancellations made more than 24 hours prior to the scheduled pickup time are eligible for a 50% refund.

Cancellations Within 24 Hours of Scheduled Service
Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled pickup time are non-refundable.

2. No-Show Policy

A no-show is defined as a customer who:

  • Fails to appear at the scheduled pickup location, or
  • Is not ready within the allowed waiting period

No-shows are non-refundable and will be charged the full fare.

3. Pickup & Waiting Policy

Customers must be ready at the scheduled pickup time.

A 15-minute grace period is provided from the scheduled pickup time.

After 15 minutes, continued waiting is not guaranteed and is at the driver’s discretion based on scheduling and the next scheduled pickup.

If the customer is not present after the grace period, the trip may be considered a no-show and is non-refundable.

Additional wait time, if available, may result in added charges.

Airport Express is not responsible for delays caused by:

  • Customer lateness
  • Inaccurate booking information
  • Traffic, weather, or unforeseen conditions
4. Pet & Animal Policy

Pets are permitted only if they weigh 25 lbs or less and are secured in an approved carrier at all times.

Only service animals as defined under applicable law are permitted and must be disclosed at the time of booking.

Service animals must be trained to perform a specific task related to a disability and must remain under control at all times. Animals that are not housebroken, disruptive, or pose a safety risk may result in refusal of service.

5. Vehicle Condition & Cleaning Fees

Customers are responsible for maintaining the condition of the vehicle during transport.

A cleaning or damage fee may be charged if:

  • Excessive mess occurs
  • Spills, stains, or odors require cleaning
  • Damage to the vehicle is caused by passengers or belongings
6. Passenger Conduct

Airport Express reserves the right to refuse or terminate service if a passenger:

  • Is disruptive or unsafe
  • Is under the influence to a degree that affects safety
  • Poses a risk to the driver, vehicle, or other passengers

In such cases, no refund will be issued.

7. Booking Agreement

By booking a service with Airport Express, the customer acknowledges and agrees to all policies outlined above.
