Asheville to Charlotte Airport Transportation

Asheville Airport Express provides private airport transportation from Asheville to Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT).

Our long-distance airport car service offers a reliable alternative to standard airport shuttle and airport taxi options for travelers heading to Charlotte.

Flat-rate CLT service is $325 total for up to 4 passengers and includes space for 4 large checked bags and 4 carry-on bags.

Service is offered by advance reservation with scheduled pickup windows between 8:00 AM and 4:00 PM.

Cancellation Policy

1. 24-Hour Risk-Free Cancellation Customers may cancel their booking within 24 hours of the original reservation time and receive a full refund, provided the scheduled trip is more than 24 hours away at the time of booking. If the trip is scheduled to occur within 24 hours of booking, the reservation is non-refundable.

2. Cancellations More Than 24 Hours Before Scheduled Service Cancellations made more than 24 hours prior to the scheduled pickup time are eligible for a 50% refund.

3. Cancellations Within 24 Hours of Scheduled Service Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled pickup time are non-refundable.
